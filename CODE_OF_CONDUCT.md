# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working with limited time.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Spam or low-effort automated contributions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, hide, or remove contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from the project.
